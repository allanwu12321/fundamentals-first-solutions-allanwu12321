Spaghetti and Meatballs

Today, we're going to cook a classic spaghetti and meatball recipe that anyone can enjoy. This simple recipe only takes 8 ingredients and you can prepare and cook it in less than 30 minutes .

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 200g
  • Ground Beef - 500g
  • Tomato Sauce - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Olive Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and Pepper - to taste
  • Parmesan Cheese - grated, for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce and let simmer for 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
